Our Mission

Building the Future of Data Infrastructure

We're creating a decentralized physical infrastructure network (DePIN) that democratizes access to real-world data while rewarding contributors fairly and maintaining privacy.

Global Coverage

Leverage the ubiquity of smartphones to create unprecedented geographic coverage for environmental and network data collection. Our goal is to provide real-time sensor data from every corner of the globe.

Privacy First

Implement cryptographic privacy guarantees through location fuzzing, data hashing, and hardware attestation. Users maintain complete control over their data while earning rewards.

Fair Compensation

Reward contributors fairly for their valuable data. Our tokenomics ensure sustainable earnings of $5-25/month per device, creating genuine passive income opportunities.

Solana Speed

Built on Solana for high-speed, low-cost transactions. Process millions of sensor readings per day with negligible fees, enabling real-time data markets.

Hardware Verified

Utilize TEEPIN attestation on Solana Seeker devices to cryptographically verify data authenticity. This makes our data 3-10x more valuable than unverified sources.

Sustainable Growth

Design economic mechanisms that ensure long-term sustainability. Balance token emissions with real demand from enterprise data buyers creating lasting value.

Strategy

Our Path to Scale

A phased approach to building the world's largest decentralized sensor network, with clear milestones and measurable objectives.

Phase 1: Foundation (Q1-Q2 2026)

Mobile App & Core Infrastructure

Launch production-ready Progressive Web App with complete sensor integration, Seed Vault wallet connectivity, and real-time data collection. Deploy smart contracts for token distribution and data verification on Solana devnet.

Target: 1,000 beta users
Goal: 1M sensor readings/day
Phase 2: Seeker Integration (Q3 2026)

Hardware Attestation & Premium Data

Full integration with Solana Seeker devices and TEEPIN attestation. Launch hardware-verified data marketplace targeting enterprise buyers. Deploy Trusted Web Activity for native app experience.

Target: 10,000 Seeker devices
Goal: 100M verified readings/month
Phase 3: Data Marketplace (Q4 2026)

Enterprise Sales & Revenue Generation

Launch B2B data marketplace with API access for telecom operators, urban planners, weather services, and research institutions. Implement tiered pricing for raw, aggregated, and premium attested data.

Target: 50 enterprise customers
Goal: $500K ARR
Phase 4: Global Scale (2027)

Network Effects & Market Leadership

Expand to 150K+ active devices globally. Achieve market leadership in decentralized sensor networks. Implement advanced analytics, machine learning insights, and predictive models as premium data products.

Target: 150K+ devices
Goal: $5M+ ARR
